# Limitations 

Statistical analysis is not conducted on these results. Though, it might be intuitvely clear that the benefit conferred upon by windowing is minimal or negligible, future work should investigate the precise relationship between windowing (or graph structure in general), topology, and preservation of knowledge in the embedding space.
